Our Story

Born in 2025 from a shared passion for fitness and technology, ZenFit was founded by five visionary Nepali professionals who saw the need for better gym management solutions. What started as late-night coding sessions and countless cups of tea has grown into Nepal's most promising fitness tech startup.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to make gym management as smooth as your best workout routine. No more paperwork piles, no more membership confusion - just clean, efficient technology that lets you focus on what really matters: fitness.
